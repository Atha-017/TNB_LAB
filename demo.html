Our inspiration

Ratan Tata


Our lab is deeply inspired by the visionary leadership and commitment to innovation exemplified by Padmashree Ratan Tata. His dedication to social impact and ethical excellence drives us to pursue groundbreaking advancements in Translational Nanomedicine and Bioengineering Lab Focus Area Nanotechnology and Precision therapy to make a meaningful difference in healthcare.
Through our work, we aim to embody his values of integrity, innovation, and empathy in all our endeavors.

Rinti Banerjee

Our lab is guided by the pioneering spirit and dedication of the late Prof. Rinti Banerjee, who served as a mentor and a true visionary in the field of Nanomedicine. Her passion for impactful, interdisciplinary research continues to inspire our work, reminding us to pursue scientific excellence with purpose and compassion. Prof. Banerjee’s legacy of curiosity and commitment lives on as we strive to push the boundaries of knowledge and innovation.
