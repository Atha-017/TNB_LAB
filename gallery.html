Celebrating Excellence

Our lab proudly celebrated a remarkable achievement as Dr. Puja Sandbhor received the Prime Minister’s Early Career Research Grant for her groundbreaking work in glioblastoma precision therapeutics. This honor recognizes our lab’s dedication to advancing 3D organoid models for studying tumor microenvironments and therapy resistance. A special thanks to ANRF, DST India, and ACTREC-TMC for their support.
